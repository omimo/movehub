Knocking and Direction Gestures with Variation form LMA
This is a multi-modal recording of Knocking and Direction Gestures to which we applied single variation in terms of Space, Effort, Shape and Phrasing form Laban Movement Analysis
Provided by: Moving Stories
Contact: Shannon Cuykendall
The data was captured at the Integrated Motion Studio at Emily Carr University of Art + Design.
